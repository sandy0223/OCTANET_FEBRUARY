Say Hi to our friendly neighbourhood Spidey

The Avengers is a popular superhero team in the Marvel Comics universe. It was created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby and made its debut in 1963. The team's core members include iconic superheroes like Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, Hulk, Black Widow, Spiderman , Hawkeye, among others. They come together to fight powerful supervillains and protect the world from various threats. The Avengers have been adapted into successful movies, forming the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), and have a significant fan following worldwide.
